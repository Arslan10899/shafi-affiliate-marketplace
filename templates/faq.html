Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions below

Orders

Simply browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will need to provide your shipping details and choose a payment method to complete the purchase.
Orders can be modified or canceled within 30 minutes of placement. Please contact our support team as soon as possible with your order number.
You will receive an order confirmation email with your order number and details shortly after placing your order. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.

Payments

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed securely.
Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. Your payment details are never stored on our servers.
Your card will be charged immediately upon placing the order. If the order is canceled, a refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Shipping

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. International shipping may take 10-15 business days depending on the destination.
Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Free express shipping is available on orders over $100.

Returns

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused and in their original packaging. Some exceptions apply for perishable goods and personalized items.
Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.
