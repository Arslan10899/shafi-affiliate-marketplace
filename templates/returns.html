Returns & Refunds

Our hassle-free return policy

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy for any reason, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligibility Conditions
  • Items must be unused and in the same condition as received.
  • Items must be returned in the original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required.
  • Certain items such as perishable goods, intimate apparel, and personalized products are not eligible.

Return Process

Step 1
Request a Return

Log into your account and submit a return request with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2
Pack the Item

Securely pack the item in its original packaging with all accessories and documentation.

Step 3
Ship It Back

Print the prepaid return label and drop off the package at the nearest shipping center.

Step 4
Refund Issued

Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Refund Timeline

Stage Estimated Time
Return shipped back to us 3-7 business days
Item inspection 1-2 business days
Refund processing 5-7 business days
Total estimated time 9-16 business days

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take additional time for the refund to appear in your account.
